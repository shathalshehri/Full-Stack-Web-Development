The exhibition "Transformation: Meet the Dinosaurs"
presents unique and fascinating designs with life-sized animated dinosaurs
for a unique experience that tells an important story.
Under the direction of archaeologist Enrico Squazini, visitors will set out on a journey
through the underwater world and forests 66 million years ago
and learn about important issues such as the environment,
time and change, which resulted from "transformation".
